About Harvey Doodle

Biography

Harvey Doodle, hailed as the cutest fluffball in New Jersey, is a lovable furball who enjoys the simple pleasures of life. Living with his two loving pawrents, every day is a new adventure for him!

From chasing his tail to basking in the sun, Harvey makes sure to live each moment to the fullest, bringing joy to everyone around him.
